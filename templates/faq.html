Helicopter Trip FAQ

Q: What is the minimum age requirement for passengers??

A:Children must be three years of age or above to be carried on any of our Flight Experience, Sightseeing or Pleasure Flights. All Children under 10 years of age must be accompanied by an adult.

Q: How do I make a reservation?

A: Reservations can be made through our Booking page. Simply choose your preferred package, select the date and time, and complete the booking process.

Q: What is the duration of the helicopter ride?

A: The duration varies depending on the package you choose. Most tours range from 30 minutes to 1 hour of flight time.

Q: Are there any age restrictions?

A: We welcome passengers of all ages. However, for safety reasons, children under the age of 2 are not permitted on board.

Q: How many passengers can the helicopter accommodate?

A: one to three passengers

Q: Can I bring bags or backpacks?

A: Due to limited cabin space, we advise passengers to bring only small bags or personal items. Larger bags and backpacks will not be allowed on board.

Q: Is there a weight limit for passengers?

A: Yes, there is a weight limit per passenger to ensure the safety and balance of the helicopter. Please refer to the specific package details for weight restrictions.

Q: What is the cancellation policy?

A: Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package and timing of cancellation. Please review our Cancellation page for detailed information.

Q: Are pets allowed on board?

A: Unfortunately, for the safety and comfort of all passengers, pets are not allowed on our helicopter tours.

Q: How early should I arrive for my flight?

A: We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before your scheduled departure time to allow for check-in and pre-flight instructions.

Q: Is tipping the pilot required?

A: While not required, if you enjoyed your flight and the service provided by our pilot, you are welcome to tip as a gesture of appreciation.

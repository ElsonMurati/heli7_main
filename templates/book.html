    Please Choose Your Destination

    Experience London City!

    Enjoy riveting aerial views of Central London on our most popular tour! The London sightseeing tour starts with a flight across the Surrey countryside north towards Central London and follows the River Thames right through the heart of the Capital. Trip of a lifetime, take in unique views of some of Central London’s most famous landmarks: Houses of Parliament, Millennium Eye, Trafalgar Square, St Paul’s, The Globe Theatre, The City, The Tower of London, HMS Belfast, Tower Bridge, the Olympic Stadium and Canary Wharf before flying over Croydon and back to our base at Redhill Aerodrome.

    Explore vibrant Kent Heritage !

    Sit back and relax as we take you on the Kent Heritage Tour! This fantastic tour covers a number of the historic sites in Kent; The Garden of England. Flight time is approximately 20-25 minutes, air traffic allowing.

    Discover Thames and Medway!

    Spend a blissful hour exploring the Thames and Medway Estuaries with amazing sights of innovative buildings and natural erosion showing the sheer power of rivers.

    Relax and enjoy Royal Surrey!

    Sit back and relax as we take you on the Royal Surrey Tour! This fantastic trip takes you over the beautiful Surrey countryside and combines scenic country views with historic buildings. Flight time is approximately 20-25 minutes, air traffic allowing. We recommend allowing an hour for the whole experience with us.
